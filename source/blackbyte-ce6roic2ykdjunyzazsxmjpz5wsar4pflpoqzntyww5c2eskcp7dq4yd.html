Contraloría General de la República

We supervise and verify the correct application of public policies and the use of State resources and assets, through our regional control departments, the Institutional Control Bodies (OCI) and the Audit Societies (SOA). Our action includes activities in the administrative, budgetary, operational and financial field of public entities, in accordance with the powers conferred on us by Law No. 27785 - Organic Law of the National Control System and the Comptroller General of the Republic and Law No. 30742 – Law to Strengthen the Comptroller General of the Republic and the National Control System. The National Control System is made up of the Comptroller General as the governing technical entity, all the Institutional Control Bodies of the entities and the external and independent Audit Companies appointed by the Comptroller General and contracted for a given period. As the Supreme Audit Institution of Peru (SAI Peru), we are members of the Steering Committee of the International Organization of Supreme Audit Institutions ( INTOSAI ) and hold the presidency of the Latin American and Caribbean Organization of Supreme Audit Institutions – OLACEFS and, We are part of the Citizen Participation Commission. This presence allows us to promote technical cooperation actions with the purpose of managing access to technologies, knowledge and experience to strengthen the development of capacities and obtain non-reimbursable resources for the benefit of the National Control System. Contraloría General de la República is located in LIMA, LIMA, Peru and is part of the Executive, Legislative, and Other General Government Support Industry. Contraloría General de la República has 4,684 total employees across all of its locations.
